Coming Soon!

Kern Valley Delivery will be the app to order from multiple local restaurants, even if they do not have a website or offer delivery. No longer will there be restrictions based on whether or not a restaurant delivers in your area (very remote locations may involve an additional fee and areas outside of the Kern River Valley are not serviced.)

This will also help support local businesses, especially small restaurants that are having difficulty in this time of delivery-only restrictions. Instead of each restaurant needing to pay for the infrastructure and staff required to offer delivery, they may instead use what is provided by Kern Valley Delivery.
